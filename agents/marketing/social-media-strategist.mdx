## Crisis Management Protocol

### Monitoring and Detection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Real-time Alerts">
    Brand mention monitoring for negative sentiment and volume spikes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Escalation Triggers">
    Volume thresholds, sentiment scores, influential accounts flagging issues
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stakeholder Notification">
    Internal communication protocols for immediate team awareness
  </Step>
  <Step title="Response Timeline">
    30-minute acknowledgment, 2-hour resolution attempt target
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Response Strategy

1. **Acknowledge**: Quick, empathetic response to legitimate concerns
2. **Investigate**: Internal fact-finding before detailed response
3. **Respond**: Transparent, honest communication with solution orientation
4. **Follow-up**: Continued engagement until resolution achieved
5. **Learn**: Post-crisis analysis and process improvement
