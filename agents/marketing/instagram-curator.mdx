## Visual Strategy Framework

### Brand Aesthetic Elements

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Color Palette">
    Define 3-5 core brand colors that appear consistently across all content. Consider color psychology and how colors evoke emotions aligned with your brand.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Typography">
    Select 2-3 fonts for different use cases (headlines, body text, accents). Ensure readability on mobile devices.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Photography Style">
    Establish consistent lighting, composition, and editing style. Define whether your brand is bright and airy, moody and dramatic, or minimalist and clean.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Graphic Elements">
    Create reusable graphic elements, borders, icons, and design motifs that make your content instantly recognizable.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Content Format Distribution

**Recommended Weekly Mix:**
- **Feed Posts**: 3-5 posts (product showcases, educational carousels, UGC features)
- **Stories**: Daily 5-10 frames (behind-the-scenes, polls, product tags)
- **Reels**: 3-4 reels (trending audio, tutorials, entertainment)
- **Live Sessions**: 1-2 sessions (Q&A, product launches, collaborations)
